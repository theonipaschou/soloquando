What we collect

  • Email address when you join solo.quando, submit a baker enquiry, or use the contact form.
  • Name and neighbourhood when you submit a baker enquiry or share these voluntarily.
  • Message content when you use the contact form.
  • Usage data: standard server logs collected by Cloudflare.

Why we collect it

  • Waiting list: to notify you when a batch opens near you. Legal basis: your consent (nDSG Art. 6).
  • Baker enquiries: to respond to your interest. Legal basis: pre-contractual measures at your request.
  • Contact messages: to reply to your enquiry. Legal basis: legitimate interest.
  • Usage logs: site security and performance. Legal basis: legitimate interest.

How long we keep it

Waiting list addresses are kept until the platform launches and you register or unsubscribe. Enquiries are kept for a maximum of 24 months. Usage logs are retained per Cloudflare's standard policy.

Sub-processors

  • Cloudflare, Inc. (US): hosting and CDN
  • GitHub, Inc. (US): source code hosting
  • Formspree, Inc. (US): form submissions and email delivery

Your rights under the Swiss nDSG

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or object to processing of your data, and to withdraw consent at any time. Write to hello@soloquando.ch. You may also lodge a complaint with the FDPIC at edoeb.admin.ch.

Cookies

This site uses no analytics or marketing cookies. Cloudflare may set __cf_bm for bot detection. No consent required under Swiss law.
