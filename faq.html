Common questions

Honest answers.

If your question isn't here, write to us at hello@soloquando.ch. We read every message.

What is solo.quando?

A demand-first pre-order marketplace for homemade food in Zürich. A baker proposes a batch. Neighbours pre-order. Nothing is made until enough people say yes.

What if a batch doesn't reach its minimum?

Every payment returns automatically, in full, every time. No exceptions, no credits, no partial refunds. The full amount, every time.

Where do I pick up the food?

At a place we keep private. Central Zürich, quiet, warm, and considered. The address is shared only once you've committed to a batch. We do not deliver. Pickup is part of the experience.

Why a secret pickup location?

Two reasons. Home bakers should not have strangers at their door. And something quietly held becomes something worth arriving for.

How is food safety handled?

Every listing must declare every ingredient and every allergen before publication. Swiss food-safety law (LMG SR 817.0 / LGV SR 817.02) applies to all producers, who are responsible for accurate declarations.

Can I bake on solo.quando?

Yes. That is what the platform is for. Tell us about your recipe through the baker form. No commercial kitchen required, no minimum volumes. We guide you through every administrative step.

Is this accessible for people with disabilities or chronic illness?

Intentionally so. Producers never need to leave their home. Pickup happens at our central address, not theirs. For neighbours, a short pre-arranged collection is significantly easier than markets or shops.

When does this launch?

We are in pre-launch in Zürich. Join the waiting list to be notified the moment a batch opens in your neighbourhood.

How is my data handled?

Personal data is processed under the Swiss nDSG. We don't sell or share your data for marketing. See the Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I get in touch?

Email hello@soloquando.ch, call +41 78 794 27 00, or use the contact form. We reply within 48 hours.
