1. Scope

These Terms govern access to and use of soloquando.ch. By joining the waiting list, submitting a baker enquiry, or otherwise using the Platform, you accept these Terms in full.

2. About solo.quando

The Platform is operated by Theoni Paschou, Frauentalweg 119, 8045 Zürich, Switzerland (see the Imprint). solo.quando is an intermediary; the contract for any food item is concluded directly between Producer and Buyer.

3. The mechanism

A Producer lists a batch with a minimum threshold. A Buyer's payment is held, not charged, until the threshold is reached before the deadline. If met, the batch is confirmed. If not, all holds are released automatically.

4. Pricing and payment

Producers set their own prices. solo.quando charges a platform fee, deducted from the Producer's payout. Buyers pay the price shown at commitment, no hidden charges. All prices in CHF and include applicable taxes.

5. Pickup

All orders are collected in person at a curated central Zürich location, the address of which is shared only with confirmed buyers. solo.quando does not provide delivery.

6. Cancellations and refunds

If a batch does not reach its minimum, all holds are released automatically and in full. If a confirmed batch is cancelled by the Producer, Buyers receive a full refund. If goods are materially different from the listing, contact hello@soloquando.ch within 24 hours of collection.

7. Food safety

Producers must declare all ingredients and allergens (LMG SR 817.0 / LGV SR 817.02). Buyers with severe allergies should contact the Producer directly before committing.

8. Liability

solo.quando is not a party to the contract between Producer and Buyer and accepts no liability for the quality, safety, or accuracy of food listed, except in cases of wilful misconduct or gross negligence. Total liability is limited to fees paid in the twelve months preceding the claim.

9. Governing law

These Terms are governed by Swiss law, excluding conflict-of-laws rules and the CISG. Exclusive jurisdiction: Zürich, subject to mandatory consumer-protection provisions.
