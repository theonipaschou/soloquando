How to review this.

Walk through each page card above. The top of each card is the top of the page. The colour band tells you what the visitor sees at that point.

If a colour feels wrong on any section, tell me which page and which section, and what colour you want. The build script applies your edits in one pass to every file.

  • What changed from before. Option B is restored. The two real bugs are fixed: pickup-s on how-it-works (was rendering paper but with terra-coloured text, invisible) and the conflicting later rules that overrode Option B. The "Be among the first" sections are gone. The unified newsletter strip sits above the dark footer on every page.
  • The rhythm rule. No two adjacent sections share the same colour theme. The previous bug (founder-s + READY-TO-START both ink) cannot recur because the structure now goes founder → cream newsletter → ink footer.
  • What's new on contact. The hero is ink, paths are cream, details are paper, then newsletter on cream, then footer on ink. Before, contact had no closing newsletter. Now it does, like every other page.
  • Bake with us, kept on purpose. The "Tell us about your recipe" section (ink) sits between handle-s and the newsletter strip. It's the page's reason to exist. The colours rotate cleanly around it.
  • Things I did not touch. Copy, images, layouts, navigation, footer content, individual section internals. Only colours, structural cleanup, and the newsletter strip insertion.
  • Things to consider next. The trust-band on index is currently paper but very thin (3 small columns). It might want to be the same as the section before or after. Also, the example-s (open batches) on index is on paper while the batch-section on products is on cream. Worth aligning if you want consistent batch backgrounds across both pages.
