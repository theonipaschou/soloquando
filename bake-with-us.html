For producers

Your recipe deserves more
than your kitchen.

You bake every week. More than your table can hold, more than your friends can eat. Somewhere nearby, five people would love what you make. They just don't know you exist.

Three kinds of bakers
who have been waiting.

The keeper of recipes

Sixty years of baking. Recipes your mother taught you, and her mother taught her. You worry they'll die with you. They don't have to.

The carrier of culture

You moved to Switzerland with a suitcase and a recipe book. Your börek, your maamoul, your Cantucci taste different here because they're made the original way. Your neighbours don't know what they're missing.

The weekend baker

You bake every Sunday because you love it. You give half away to colleagues on Monday. You've thought about selling, but you don't want a business — you just want to find the people waiting for what you make.

5

You never bake for nothing.

The hardest part of selling homemade food is not knowing who's buying. You spend a Sunday baking thirty jars of jam. You sell twelve. Eighteen go stale.

solo.quando works the other way. You propose a batch. You set the minimum — usually five people. You don't touch the oven until five people have paid. If they don't commit, the batch closes, nobody is charged, and you haven't lost a Sunday or a gram of flour.

Baking is better when someone is waiting for it.

You bring the recipe.
We handle the rest.

Pricing guidance

We help you price your batch to reflect the real value of your time and ingredients. Most home bakers systematically underprice. We won't let that happen.

Packaging standards

What to put it in, how to label it, what buyers need to see. A template and a checklist. You don't need to figure this out yourself.

The administrative steps

Ingredient declaration, allergen listing, what Swiss food-safety law requires. We guide you through exactly what's needed and no more.

Payments and payouts

Payments handled through the platform. Your payout arrives when the batch completes. Refunds are automatic — nothing for you to manage.

Discovery and matching

We surface your batch to buyers in your neighbourhood. You don't need to market yourself or build a following.

Your story, your terms

You set the minimum, the price, the pickup date. Your listing tells your story. We don't anonymise you. We introduce you.

Tell us about
your recipe.

A fifteen-minute conversation. No commitment, no paperwork. We want to understand what you make, why it matters, and whether solo.quando is the right place for it.

No commercial kitchen required. No minimum volume. No logistics to solve alone. If you can make it, we help you share it.

solo.quando is applying to the Innovation Sandbox of the Canton of Zürich — developing the regulatory framework for home-baker platforms with the regulator in the room. The first bakers who join do so as participants in something genuinely new.
