For producers

Your recipe deserves
more than your kitchen.

You bake every week. More than your table can hold. Five neighbours nearby would love what you make. They just don't know you exist yet.

Who we built this for

For the bakers who were
never asked.

Most food platforms were built around professional kitchens, delivery logistics, and volume. solo.quando was built around a person with a recipe and the neighbours who want it.

For the keeper of recipes whose mother taught her to bake. For the carrier of culture whose food does not appear on any Swiss shelf. For the home-bound maker whose kitchen is her best asset. For the weekend baker who bakes every Sunday because she loves it. And for anyone in between.

You don't need a business plan. You don't need to pretend you are something you are not. You only need a recipe, and the willingness to share it.

The shield

You never bake for nothing.

You propose a batch. You set the minimum number of yeses you need. You don't touch the oven until enough people have committed.

If they don't commit, the batch closes. Nobody is charged. You haven't lost a Sunday or a gram of flour.

Baking is better when it's wanted.

∞

A pickup that protects you

You don't open your home.

Every pickup happens at a single, central Zürich address we curate. Quiet, warm, and considered. Strangers never come to your door.

You drop off your batch when you are ready, neighbours collect from the same place. The address is private until people have committed. You stay in your kitchen, on your terms.

It is one of the things we built carefully on purpose. Especially for home bakers, the home-bound, and anyone who values their privacy.

Less to figure out

You bring the recipe.
We handle the rest.

Pricing guidance

Most home bakers systematically underprice. We help you reflect the real value of your time.

Packaging standards

What to put it in, how to label it. A template and a checklist, no experience needed.

Administrative steps

Ingredient declaration, allergen listing, what Swiss food-safety law requires. We guide you through every step.

Payments handled

Payments go through the platform. Your payout arrives when the batch completes. Refunds are automatic.

Discovery and matching

The platform matches your batch to neighbours nearby. No following needed, no audience to build.

Your story, your terms

You set the minimum, the price, the pickup window. We introduce you. We don't anonymise you.

Our promise to producers

A good photo of your batch, on us.

If you don't have a good picture of what you make, we'll help you take one. A platform is only as warm as the photographs on it. We treat your batch the way it deserves to be seen.
