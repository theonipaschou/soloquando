For producers

Your recipe deserves
more than your kitchen.

You bake every week. More than your table can hold. Five people nearby would love what you make — they just don't know you exist yet.

The bakers we were built for.

Most food platforms were designed around professional kitchens. solo.quando was designed around a person with a recipe and five neighbours who want it.

👵
The keeper of recipes

Decades of baking. Recipes your mother taught you. You worry they'll die with you. They don't have to.

🌍
The carrier of culture

You moved here with a recipe book. Your börek, your maamoul taste different — made the original way. Your neighbours don't know what they're missing.

🏠
The home-bound baker

Whether because of disability, illness, or caregiving — your kitchen is your best asset. Pickup happens at your door. You never need to leave home to run a batch.

🌿
The weekend baker

You bake every Sunday because you love it. You don't want a business — just the five people already waiting for what you make.

5

You never bake for nothing.

You propose a batch. You set the minimum — usually five people. You don't touch the oven until five people have paid.

If they don't commit, the batch closes. Nobody is charged. You haven't lost a Sunday or a gram of flour.

Baking is better when someone is waiting for it.

You bring the recipe.
We handle the rest.

Pricing guidance

Most home bakers systematically underprice. We help you price to reflect the real value of your time.

Packaging standards

What to put it in, how to label it. A template and a checklist — no experience needed.

Administrative steps

Ingredient declaration, allergen listing, what Swiss food-safety law requires. We guide you through it.

Payments and payouts

Payments handled through the platform. Your payout arrives when the batch completes.

Discovery and matching

The platform matches your batch to buyers in your neighbourhood. No following needed.

Your story, your terms

You set the minimum, the price, the pickup date. We introduce you — we don't anonymise you.

Tell us about
your recipe.

A fifteen-minute conversation. No commitment, no paperwork. We want to understand what you make and whether solo.quando is the right place for it.

We are in a pilot phase — the first fifty bakers will shape everything that follows.

solo.quando is applying to the Innovation Sandbox of the Canton of Zürich — co-developing the regulatory framework for home-baker platforms with the regulator in the room.
