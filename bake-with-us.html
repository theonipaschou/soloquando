For bakers

Your recipe deserves
more than your kitchen.

You bake every week. More than your table can hold. The neighbours nearby would love what you make.

Who we built this for

For the bakers who were
never asked.

Most food platforms were built around professional kitchens, delivery logistics, and volume.

solo.quando was built around a person with a recipe and the neighbours who want it.

You don't need a business plan. You don't need to pretend you are something you are not. You only need a recipe, and the willingness to share it.

The shield

You never bake
for nothing.

You propose a batch (a small round of baking, your recipe, your terms). You set the minimum number of yeses you need.

You don't touch the oven until enough people have committed. If they don't, the batch closes. Nobody is charged. You haven't lost a Sunday or a gram of flour.

Baking is better when it's wanted.

A curated pickup point

One quiet place to meet.

Every pickup happens at a single, central Zürich address we curate. Quiet, warm, and considered.

You drop your batch off when you're ready. Neighbours collect from the same place.

The food is homemade. The handover should feel that way too.

A clear division of labour

You bring the food.
We handle the rest.

You bake. You cook. You make. We take care of everything around it.
