Who we built this for

The bakers who were
never asked.

Most food platforms were built around professional kitchens, delivery logistics, and volume. solo.quando was built around a person with a recipe and the five neighbours who want it.

The keeper of recipes

Decades of baking. Recipes your mother taught you. You worry they'll die with you. They don't have to.

🌍

The carrier of culture

You moved here with a recipe book. Your börek, your maamoul, your Cantucci taste different — made the original way. Your neighbours don't know what they're missing.

🏠

The home-bound baker

Whether because of disability, illness, or caregiving — your kitchen is your best asset. Pickup happens at your door. You never need to leave home.

🌿

The weekend baker

You bake every Sunday because you love it. You don't want a business — just the people already waiting for what you make.

5

The shield

You never bake for nothing.

You propose a batch. You set the minimum. You don't touch the oven until enough people have committed.

If they don't commit, the batch closes. Nobody is charged. You haven't lost a Sunday or a gram of flour.

Baking is better when someone is waiting for it.

Less to figure out

You bring the recipe.
We handle the rest.

Pricing guidance

Most home bakers systematically underprice. We help you reflect the real value of your time.

Packaging standards

What to put it in, how to label it. A template and a checklist — no experience needed.

Administrative steps

Ingredient declaration, allergen listing, what Swiss food-safety law requires. We guide you through it.

Payments handled

Payments go through the platform. Your payout arrives when the batch completes. Refunds are automatic.

Discovery and matching

The platform matches your batch to buyers in your neighbourhood. No following needed.

Your story, your terms

You set the minimum, the price, the pickup. We introduce you — we don't anonymise you.
