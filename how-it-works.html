The mechanic

Three quiet steps.

Nothing is made until enough people say yes. Nothing is wasted. A real person, a real recipe.

Step one

She opens a batch.

A baker proposes what she wants to make this week. Her recipe, her price, her pickup date. Every ingredient and allergen listed.

A proposal — not a product. Nothing on a shelf, nothing made yet.

Step two

You say yes.

Before anything is made. Your payment is held — never taken — until the kitchen opens.

If enough people don't commit before the deadline, every Franc returns to you. Automatically. Always.

Step three

Five yeses. Enough.

When enough people say yes, she turns on the oven. You collect from her door, at a time you both agree.

You meet the person who made your food. That is not a limitation — it is the point.

Our promises

Three things, always true.

Full refund. Always.

If a batch doesn't reach its minimum, every Franc comes back automatically. No conditions, no process.

Every ingredient declared.

Allergens and ingredients listed before you commit. No surprises, no hidden anything.

Real names, real kitchens.

No anonymous accounts. You meet the person who made your food.
