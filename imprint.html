Platform description

solo.quando is a demand-first pre-order marketplace connecting home bakers ("Producers") with buyers ("Buyers") in Switzerland. The platform operates at soloquando.ch. Theoni Paschou acts as an intermediary; the contract for any food item is concluded directly between Producer and Buyer.

Legal form

Einzelunternehmen under Swiss law. GmbH registration is planned after initial traction is established.

Food safety

All food listed on solo.quando is subject to the Swiss Federal Act on Foodstuffs and Utility Articles (LMG, SR 817.0) and the Swiss Food and Utility Articles Ordinance (LGV, SR 817.02). Every listing must declare all ingredients and allergens before publication. Producers are responsible for compliance with all applicable Swiss food-safety regulations.
