01
A baker proposes a batch

Name, recipe, price, pickup date. Every ingredient listed. Minimum set at five people.

02
You say yes — before they cook

Your payment is held, not charged, until five people commit. If five don't sign up, every Franc returns to you. Automatically.

03
Pickup near their front door

Ten minutes, agreed time. You meet the person who made your food. That's not a limitation — it's the point.
