Why this exists

Most homemade food
never finds its people.

Across Switzerland, 2.8 million tonnes of food are wasted every year. 38% comes from households alone. Most of it baked with care, by people who simply made too much.

solo.quando reverses the order. Ask first. Bake second.

Common questions

Honest answers.

When does this launch?

solo.quando is live in Zürich. Batch 002 (Moustokouloura) was completed on Saturday 16 May. Batches 003 (Ergolavoi) and 005 (L’Osanna) are open now — pre-order by Friday 22 May, 12:00. See the current batches →

What if a batch doesn't reach its threshold?

Every payment returns automatically and in full. No exceptions, no credits, no partial refunds.

How does pickup work?

Pickup happens at a single, central Zürich address we curate. Quiet, warm, and considered. The address is shared once you've committed.

Is the food safe? What about allergens?

Every listing must declare every ingredient and every allergen. Swiss food-safety law (LMG SR 817.0) applies to all bakers.
