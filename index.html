Common questions

Honest answers.

The four most common questions before launch.

When does this launch?

First batches in Zürich are planned for May 2026. Join the waiting list and you'll be the first to hear the moment a baker in your neighbourhood is ready.

What if a batch doesn't reach its threshold?

Every payment is released back to you, automatically and in full. No exceptions, no credits, no partial refunds. The full amount, every time.

How does pickup work?

Pickup happens at a place we keep private. Central Zürich, quiet, warm, and considered. The address is shared only once you've committed to a batch. We do not deliver.

Is the food safe? What about allergens?

Every listing must declare every ingredient and every allergen before publication. Swiss food-safety law (LMG SR 817.0 / LGV SR 817.02) applies to all bakers, who are responsible for accurate declarations.
