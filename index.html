Common questions

Honest answers.

When does this launch?

solo.quando is live in Zürich. Batch 003 (Mousto) is open for pre-orders now. See the current batches →

What if a batch doesn't reach its threshold?

Every payment returns automatically and in full. No exceptions, no credits, no partial refunds.

How does pickup work?

Pickup happens at a single, central Zürich address we curate. Quiet, warm, and considered. The address is shared once you've committed.

Is the food safe? What about allergens?

Every listing must declare every ingredient and every allergen. Swiss food-safety law (LMG SR 817.0) applies to all bakers.
